Deepline Metal-Glass Style Language

## 0) Essence

* **Mood:** cold, surgical, quiet power. Zero warmth.
* **Material:** glass–metal hybrid (“smoked chrome”): translucent edges + glossy metallic body.
* **Signature:** bright **Fresnel rim**, dimmer interior, tight **bloom** only on hottest edges.

---

## 1) Core Palette (tokens)

| Token              | Hex                | Use                     |
| ------------------ | ------------------ | ----------------------- |
| `--dl-highlight-1` | `#E9F1F6`          | peak edge/specular hits |
| `--dl-highlight-2` | `#CFE0EA`          | rolling edge into body  |
| `--dl-silver-1`    | `#A6B7C3`          | body mid-high           |
| `--dl-silver-2`    | `#8C9AA6`          | body mid-low            |
| `--dl-shadow-1`    | `#47525B`          | cool shadow             |
| `--dl-shadow-2`    | `#2B3238`          | deep cavity             |
| `--dl-bloom`       | `#DDEAF3` @ 10–18% | rim bloom/outer glow    |
| `--dl-bg`          | `#000000`          | background              |

**Contrast target:** ~**6:1** overall. Keep mids subdued so highlights read metallic.

**Default edge gradient:** `#2B3238 → #8C9AA6 → #A6B7C3 → #47525B`.

---

## 2) Finish & Texture

* **Gloss:** high; **Roughness 0.08–0.14** (no mirror).
* **Translucency:** light; edges feel thin.
* **Micro-texture:** ultra-fine satin/mesh **6–12%** visibility; only inside turns/cavities.

---

## 3) Lighting Language

* **Key Rim:** cool white from **upper-left/front**; grazing angle; strongest source.
* **Counter Rim:** **upper-right/back**, 40–60% of key.
* **Fill:** soft, below/center; just lifting interior (avoid warm casts).
* **Bloom/Glare:** apply only to top **1–2%** brightest; radius small and clean.
* **Background:** absolute black with faint vignette; object sits **8–12%** below center.

---

## 4) 3D Shader Targets (PBR shorthand)

* **Base:** `#9CAFB9`, **Metallic 0.20–0.35**, **Specular 0.6–0.8**, **Roughness 0.08–0.14**
* **IOR:** 1.45–1.50; **Transmission 0.25–0.35** (edge translucency)
* **Clearcoat:** 0.25 (Roughness 0.03)
* **Anisotropy:** 0.2 (ribbon sheen)
* **Normals:** fine noise + mesh (strength 0.05–0.10)
* **Fresnel:** explicit rim boost layer.

---

## 5) 2D Execution Rules

**Required layers (top→bottom):**

1. **Rim Bloom** (Screen, 10–18%, blur 6–12px)
2. **Edge Highlights** (Screen, blur 2–4px)
3. **Body Gradient** (Normal; use edge gradient above) + **Inner Glow** (cool white) + **Satin** (deep blue-gray)
4. **Interior Shadow** (Multiply, soft)

**Figma shortcuts:**

* Fill = Linear/Angular gradient with tokens.
* 2× **Inner Shadow** (cool white subtle + deep gray broad) for Fresnel + cavity.
* **Outer Glow** = `--dl-bloom` 8–12%, blur 6–10.
* Micro-texture overlay ≤10% (Soft Light/Multiply), clipped.

---

## 6) Form & Layout

* **Cross-section:** elliptical ribbon; slightly flatter on inside curves.
* **Thickness:** subtle variance (thicker lobes, thinner neck/crossover).
* **Twist:** near-zero (≤10° at crossover max).
* **Framing:** symbol ≈ **70–75%** canvas width; generous top negative space.

---

## 7) Wordmark Treatment

* **Finish:** **matte-silver** variant (quieter than symbol): Metallic ~0.05 / Roughness ~0.20 (or equivalent 2D).
* **Highlighting:** single top rim; minimal bloom. Symbol must outshine type.
* **Kerning/weight:** preserve brand metrics; add subtle inner shadow for seat-in-scene.

---

## 8) Motion (optional)

* **Edge run:** 0→100% sweep of highlight along rim (2–3s ease-in-out).
* **Breathing bloom:** ±5% intensity at peaks; never pulses the whole object.
* **Parallax:** micro shift (1–2px) on hover to suggest depth.

---

## 9) Guardrails (Do / Don’t)

**Do**

* Keep mids dark → let edges do the work.
* Use cool whites only; keep temperature consistent.
* Keep micro-texture nearly imperceptible.

**Don’t**

* Don’t raise midtones (plastic look).
* Don’t add warm glows or color tints.
* Don’t over-bloom or soften edges globally.

---

## 10) Deliverables

* **Master**: 4096×4096 PNG (16-bit) on black + transparent.
* **Web**: 2048, 1024 PNG; scalable **SVG** (gradients + masks).
* **Dark-mode only** by default; include pure-white flat mark for non-photoreal contexts.

This is the compact source of truth for “silvery, glowy, sleek” Deepline visuals.
