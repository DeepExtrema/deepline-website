How it Works

1

Connect your stack

Plug in your existing tools and clouds, MLflow, W&B, ClearML, AWS, GCP, or on-prem — in minutes. Deepline connects through lightweight adapters, no rewrites or lock-ins.

2

Activate agents

Choose from early playbooks like Drift Sentinel, Data Integrity Agent, or Hotfix Autopilot. Each agent observes your pipelines and learns from live metrics.

3

Let it run itself

Agents act autonomously, blocking bad deploys, triggering retrains, opening PRs, or pinging on-call when something breaks. You stay in control, but the system handles the grind.
